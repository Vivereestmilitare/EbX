Our website uses local storage to save your preferences for language and theme (light/dark mode). These are technically necessary to provide the service you have requested and are stored based on § 25(2) TTDSG. We also use a cookie to store your consent decision regarding optional features. Plausible Analytics

If you provide your consent ("Allow Optional Features"), we use the privacy-friendly web analysis service Plausible Analytics. Plausible does not use cookies and does not collect any personal data. All data, such as referral sources, top pages, and device information, is collected anonymously. The processing is based on your consent (Art. 6(1)(a) GDPR and § 25(1) TTDSG). This site uses external libraries (Tailwind CSS, Feather Icons, Vanta.js) hosted on CDNs (e.g., Cloudflare, JSDelivr). When you access our site, your browser requests these files, sending your IP address to the CDN provider. This is based on our legitimate interest (Art. 6(1)(f) GDPR) in a fast, secure, and efficient delivery of our website content.
